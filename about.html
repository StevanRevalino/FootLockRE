About Us

What is FootLockRE?

FootLockRE is a leading shoe company dedicated to providing customers with quality products and the latest styles. With a focus on innovation and quality, we pride ourselves on providing comfortable, durable shoes for your active lifestyle. Since our founding in 2005, we have quickly gained a good reputation in the local shoe market and expanded our sales network nationwide. Our philosophy revolves around our commitment to customer satisfaction and we believe that every step and detail is important in creating an unforgettable experience.

At FootLockRE, we understand that your needs are constantly evolving. That's why we work closely with our talented design team and trusted manufacturers to create shoes that seamlessly blend comfort, style and quality. In addition to providing top-notch products, we also focus on social and environmental responsibility. We strive to operate our business responsibly, consider our environmental impact, and support sustainable initiatives. By maintaining the quality of our products and reducing our environmental impact, we want to make a positive contribution to society and the planet. As we continue to prioritize innovation, FootLockRE remains committed to meeting our customers' evolving needs and being a trusted footwear partner.
